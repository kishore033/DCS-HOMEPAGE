About Us

In a fast-paced world, where technologies constantly demand enhancement and solutions, DCS SASTRA is a paramount initiative of Google Developer Student Clubs and Sastra Deemed University to promote technology and develop ingenious solutions for the betterment of local businesses and the community.

We are a group of passionate programmers who strive to help like-minded students connect, learn and grow by bridging the gap between theory and practice while constantly improving ourselves. As a means to foster peer-to-peer learning, we host study jams, tech talks, webinars, interactive workshops, and hackathons throughout the year.

We have an interesting and inventive itinerary for tech enthusiasts at our University. Connect and join the community to actively contribute towards various initiatives and to kindle and nurture the techie inside you.

We help students bridge the gap between theory and practice and grow their knowledge by providing a peer-to-peer learning environment, by conducting workshops, study jams, etc.
